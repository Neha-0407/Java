The system should support:

multiple floors
different vehicle types
parking spot allocation
ticket generation
vehicle exit
fee calculation

I have a few clarification questions before I start designing:
    1. What vehicle types should we support? (Car, bike, truck, etc.)
    2. Does each parking spot support specific vehicle types?
    3. Should parking allocation happen automatically or manually?
    4. Do we need pricing and fee calculation?
    5. Should we support multiple parking floors?
    6. Do we need reservation support?
    7. Is persistence required, or can we keep this in-memory for now?
    8. Should we consider concurrent access by multiple users?

Since multiple users may try to park or exit vehicles simultaneously, we should think about concurrency and thread safety.

For this implementation, I’ll keep persistence in-memory unless we want to extend the discussion to databases later.

From a consistency standpoint, we should ensure that a parking spot cannot be assigned to multiple vehicles concurrently, so atomic operations around allocation/deallocation are important.

Latency requirements are not extremely strict here, but correctness and consistency of spot allocation are critical.
